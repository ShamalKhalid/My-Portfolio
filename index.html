About Me

Hi, I’m Shamal Khalid N P — a Web Developer and IT Auditor at TCS.

I build clean, responsive websites that focus on usability, performance, and clarity. I’ve worked on multiple freelance and personal projects using HTML, CSS, React, and basic backend concepts, helping individuals and businesses establish a strong online presence.

Alongside development, I work as an IT Auditor at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), where I’m involved in governance checks, control assessments, risk analysis, and coordination with stakeholders (SPOCs). This role has strengthened my understanding of security, compliance, and how real-world systems are evaluated for risk and reliability—which directly influences how I approach development.

I enjoy turning requirements into practical solutions, keeping things simple, secure, and effective. Whether it’s a business website or a growing web application, I focus on building solutions that actually work.
